About the Crypt partner program

How this works

Welcome to the Crypt partner program, where you can promote your product or service to our users. By partnering with us, you pay a small fee to have your ad displayed on our website. In return, we promote your product or service to our users. This is a great way to reach a targeted audience interested in privacy and security, without using a third-party ad network like Google Ads.

Benefits

By partnering with us, you will get:

  • Exposure to a targeted audience interested in privacy and security
  • A simple and transparent pricing model
  • Custom ad design options to fit your brand
  • Featured placement in our credits for additional visibility

Pricing

We are very honest and upfront with our pricing and benefits. We offer a simple pricing model based on the type of ad you want to run:

Standard Ad: $2/week

This includes a banner ad on the homepage and nothing else. Great for attracting visitors' attention.

You will have to provide your own 728x90px image and link for the ad.

+ Ad Design: Additional $2

We will design a custom ad for you based on your requirements. This includes creating a 728x90px banner ad that fits your brand and message.

You will have to provide a logo (Optional slogan) and link for the ad. As well as anything else you want to include in the ad.

Standard Credits: $3/week

Get featured as a 'sponsor' in our credits

Premium Credits: $5/week

Get featured as a 'premium sponsor' in our credits

Custom Arrangements: Upwards of ~$10/week or more

We can organise a custom arrangement for you based on your requirements.
